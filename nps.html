The Four Pillars of Learning

Foundational Principles for Holistic Education

The four pillars of learning are fundamental principles for reshaping education to provide complete understanding and develop individuals to their fullest potential.

Learning to Know

To provide the complete understanding of a better comprehension of the world and its complexities, and to provide an appropriate and adequate foundation for future learning.

Learning to Do

To develop well-rounded skills to enable individuals to develop to their fullest practical potential, effectively applying knowledge to real-world situations.

Learning to Live Together

To create individuals who understand democratic principles, transitional understanding and impact, and respect all community and human rights to enable individual and academic links to pursue and improve society.

Learning to Be

To develop the complete potential of each individual - mind, body, intelligence, sensitivity, aesthetic appreciation, and spiritual values - to become complete persons.

Four Cardinals of Discipline

  • Give up the negative and focus on positive growth
  • Understand what is crucial for personal development
  • Take pride in achievements while maintaining humility
  • Make the most of available resources and opportunities

Uniqueness of the School

National Public School, Shivamogga uniquely blends tradition with innovation, offering a nurturing space where academic brilliance meets character-building, With a focus on creativity, critical thinking, and global awareness, the school fosters well-rounded individuals.

Its serene campus, modern amenities, and values-driven approach create an environment where every child feels inspired to grow, lead, and thrive in a changing world.
