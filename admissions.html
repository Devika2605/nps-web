Admission Policy

Admission is open to all citizens subject to rules of the management. Admission is open to lower levels within and class L. Admission to other classes will be on merit and will depend upon the sports available.

Prospectus and application for registration is available on payment at the School Office from 8:00 am to 4:30 pm

Fee Structure

The school is self-sustaining institution. The fee structure is expected to meet the development and recurring expenditure like salary of the staff. The management has adopted the policy of collecting the minimum required fee.

Documents Required

Records to be submitted along with the application form:

  • Latest passport size photograph of the student
  • Photograph of the parent with the student (3.5cm x 3.5cm)
  • Photocopy of birth certificate (attested)
  • Photocopy of the result of the previous class (attested) (for classes 2-8 only)
  • In case the registration form is filled by guardians other than parents, it should be clearly attached
  • Photocopy of the student's Aadhar card (Mandatory)
  • Address Proof - Pan card/Driving license/Electricity Bill

Admission Process: A basic admission test will be conducted on the specified date for eligibility of the particular class. Only those candidates who clear the written test will be called for an interview. Both parents are required to be present at the time of interview. The admission would be confirmed only after the final interview.

Note: Incomplete or illegible Registration Forms, without photos will not be accepted.
