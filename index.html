About NPS Shivamogga

Established in 2009, National Public School (NPS) Shimoga is a CBSE-affiliated co-educational institution offering classes from LKG to Grade 10. The school blends traditional values with modern teaching methods to ensure academic excellence and holistic growth.

With a vision to empower future generations, NPS focuses on nurturing ethical responsibility, critical thinking, and global citizenship. The campus features advanced facilities including science and computer labs, a well-stocked library, and sports infrastructure.

Through a rich mix of academics and co-curricular activities, NPS Shimoga develops confident, compassionate, and future-ready leaders prepared to thrive in a dynamic world.
