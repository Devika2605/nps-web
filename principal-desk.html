Words from Our Principal

Principal's Message

“Education is the silent architect of destiny—moulding minds, awakening souls, and building bridges to a brighter world.”

A child’s life is like a blank page—every experience, every interaction leaves a lasting mark. Children absorb the world around them like little sponges, and every word, action, or emotion from adults influences their growth in subtle yet powerful ways. Teachers, with the perfect mix of care, creativity, and challenges, play a transformative role. They are not just educators, but mentors, sculptors of character, and architects of future dreams.

Parents, on the other hand, lay the foundation. Within the warmth of a family, children learn values, emotional intelligence, and the ability to think and feel deeply. While there is no single rulebook for raising children, the most meaningful gifts we can offer them are respect, compassion, self-belief, and the freedom to follow their dreams. When parents and teachers walk together as a team, they create a nurturing ecosystem where children truly thrive.

NPS – Shivamogga provides its students safe, nurturing environment where faculty and staff "go the extra mile" to meet the needs of the students. Apart from inspiring students to achieve the best possible academic results they are encouraged to acquire habits of curiosity, mental flexibility, self-discipline and maintain openness of mind, dignity of conduct and mutual respect in the background of social, cultural and linguistic diversity. We the teachers community welcome our students to the abode of creative and innovative learning which radiates learning process in various fields of life.

At NPS – Shivamogga, we are proud to provide a safe, stimulating environment where students are encouraged to grow academically, emotionally, and socially. Our dedicated faculty goes beyond the classroom to instill curiosity, discipline, openness, and respect in every learner. On behalf of the management and teaching community, we extend a warm welcome to all parents. Together, let us guide and celebrate the journey of every child in this beautiful adventure of learning.

"True education kindles the light within—shaping not just scholars, but compassionate leaders for tomorrow's world."

– Mrs. Naveena M Pais
Principal, NPS- Shivamogga
